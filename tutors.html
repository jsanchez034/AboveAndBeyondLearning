Above & Beyond learning is continuously in search of qualified tutors to work with our students. All tutors are state certified teachers with the tools needed to be effective on each students needs . All of our tutors are processed through background checks which they must clear as well as understand and sign the Jessica Lunsford Act affidavit. Our tutors have years of teaching experience in which our program has based its success. We strive to teach and motivate each student, from those who are struggling one some subject areas to those that struggle with multiple subjects.

We have helped our students through the stress and uncertainty they have to face when taking a test like the FCAT. They are taught strengths and mechanisms that they should focus on when taking any test.

Above and Beyond Learning also focuses on the SAT and ACT.
